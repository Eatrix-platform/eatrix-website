Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to EATRIX Technologies LLP ("EATRIX", "we", "our", or "us"). We operate a food delivery platform connecting customers, restaurants, and delivery partners across Hyderabad and other cities in India.

We understand that your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains in plain language:

  • What personal information we collect and why
  • How we use and protect that information
  • With whom we share it — and the strict limits we place on that sharing
  • Your rights as a user of our platform

By using the EATRIX mobile application or website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our platform. We are committed to handling your data responsibly, transparently, and in accordance with applicable Indian data protection principles, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

This policy applies to all users of our platform — customers placing orders, restaurant partners fulfilling them, and delivery partners completing deliveries.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver our services. Below is a clear breakdown of what we collect from each group of users.

2.1 Personal Data

We collect basic personal details that help us create and manage your account:

  • Full name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile photograph (optional)

For restaurant partners, we also collect business name, FSSAI licence number, and authorised contact person details. For delivery partners, we collect government-issued ID proof and vehicle information for onboarding and compliance purposes.

2.2 Location Data

Location data is central to how EATRIX works. Here is exactly how we use it:

  • Customers: We collect your delivery address and, if you permit, real-time device location to suggest nearby restaurants and provide accurate delivery tracking.
  • Delivery partners: We collect live GPS location while a delivery is active. This data is used solely to match assignments, update customers on delivery progress, and calculate route efficiency.
  • Restaurants: We use the registered address to show your listing to nearby customers.

You can disable location permissions on your device at any time, though some features (such as "Near Me" search and live tracking) may not function without it.

2.3 Transaction Data

To process and record orders, we collect:

  • Order items, quantities, and special instructions
  • Order timestamps and delivery address
  • Order status updates and delivery confirmation
  • Payment method type (e.g., UPI, card, wallet) — we do NOT store full card numbers or CVV codes. All payment processing is handled by certified third-party payment gateways.
  • Partial transaction references (e.g., last 4 digits of card, UPI handle) for order history and refund purposes

2.4 Device and App Usage Data

To improve our platform and diagnose technical issues, we automatically collect:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and network type (Wi-Fi / mobile data)
  • App usage patterns — screens visited, time spent, features used
  • Crash reports and error logs

This technical data does not directly identify you and is used in aggregated, anonymised form for platform analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for specific, legitimate purposes. We never use your data for advertising by third parties or sell it to any external companies.

3.1 Order Processing

Your personal and order data is used to:

  • Accept, confirm, and communicate your orders
  • Transmit order details to the relevant restaurant
  • Assign a delivery partner to your order
  • Send you real-time notifications (order confirmed, being prepared, out for delivery, delivered)
  • Generate digital receipts and invoices

3.2 Delivery Tracking

We use location data from delivery partners to:

  • Provide you with a live map showing your order's progress
  • Estimate accurate delivery times
  • Optimise delivery routes to reduce wait times
  • Confirm successful delivery at your address

3.3 Customer Support

When you contact our support team, we use your account information, order history, and communication records to:

  • Identify your account and the relevant order quickly
  • Investigate and resolve complaints, disputes, and refund requests
  • Maintain a record of resolutions for quality improvement

3.4 Fraud Prevention and Safety

We analyse device data, transaction patterns, and account behaviour to:

  • Detect and prevent fraudulent orders, fake accounts, or payment abuse
  • Protect delivery partners from unsafe delivery locations
  • Flag suspicious activity on restaurant partner accounts

Our fraud detection systems use automated logic with human review for any enforcement action taken against an account.

3.5 Platform Improvement

We use aggregated, anonymised usage data to:

  • Identify and fix bugs or crashes
  • Improve the speed and reliability of the app
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users
  • Plan new features and improvements

Where we conduct A/B testing or feature experiments, we do so with anonymised user cohorts and never use it to discriminate between users in pricing or service quality.

3.6 Pricing and Order Data

When you place an order, EATRIX uses your order details, delivery address, and location data to calculate the total amount payable. This total is displayed to you before you confirm payment and includes the item subtotal, applicable taxes, and the delivery fee.

Here is how pricing data is handled across the platform:

  • Total payable amount: The delivery fee shown to customers is a single bundled figure. Internal cost components — such as platform fees, distance-based calculations, or partner payouts — are not disclosed separately to customers or third parties.
  • Restaurant payouts: Restaurants receive only the information relevant to their payout — the order amount due to them — and do not receive details of the delivery fee or any other platform cost component.
  • Delivery partner earnings: Delivery partners receive only the information needed to complete their assignment (pickup location, drop location, and route). Their earnings for a trip are communicated separately through the partner app and are not derived from or disclosed to customers.
  • Pricing logic: EATRIX does not share its internal pricing algorithms, cost models, or fee structures with any third party, except where strictly required for operational purposes (e.g., passing transaction totals to payment gateways, or providing anonymised aggregate data to analytics providers).

We are committed to pricing transparency with our customers. The total amount you see at checkout is always the final amount you pay — there are no hidden charges applied after you confirm your order.

EATRIX reserves the right to optimise pricing, delivery allocation, and operational costs through automated systems and internal algorithms. These systems are designed to improve service efficiency, reduce delivery times, and maintain platform reliability. They do not use your personal data to charge you differently from other customers for the same service, and any optimisation that affects what you pay will always be reflected in the total shown to you before checkout.

4. Sharing of Information

We follow a strict need-to-know principle. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Sharing occurs only in the limited circumstances described below, and only the minimum necessary data is shared.

4.1 With Restaurants

When you place an order, the fulfilling restaurant receives only:

  • Your first name (for a personalised experience)
  • Order items, quantities, and customisation notes
  • Estimated pickup time

Restaurants do NOT receive your phone number, full address, email, payment details, or order history from other restaurants.

4.2 With Delivery Partners

The assigned delivery partner receives only what is needed to complete the delivery:

  • Your first name
  • Delivery address or real-time drop-pin
  • Your phone number (only for the duration of the active delivery, to coordinate arrival)
  • Restaurant pickup location

Once a delivery is completed, the delivery partner's access to your personal information is automatically revoked. Delivery partners do NOT receive your email, payment details, or order history.

4.3 With Payment Gateways

Payments are processed by certified third-party payment service providers (such as Razorpay, PayU, or similar RBI-compliant gateways). We share only what the gateway requires to process the specific transaction:

  • Transaction amount and order reference ID
  • Payment method selected by the user

EATRIX does not receive or store your full card numbers, CVV, or net banking credentials. Payment processing is handled entirely by the third-party gateway — EATRIX only receives a confirmation of payment success or failure, along with a transaction reference ID for order reconciliation. All payment data is governed by the gateway’s own PCI-DSS compliant security infrastructure.

4.4 Pricing and Internal Cost Data

EATRIX does not share its internal pricing logic, delivery fee components, or cost breakdown with any external party, except as strictly required to operate the platform. Specifically:

  • Payment gateways receive only the total transaction amount and an order reference. They do not receive any breakdown of how that amount is calculated.
  • Analytics providers receive only anonymised, aggregated data (e.g., average order values, delivery time statistics). No individual pricing records are shared.
  • Restaurants receive only their payout amount. They do not have visibility into the delivery fee, platform commission structure, or any other pricing component beyond what is explicitly agreed in their partner agreement.
  • Delivery partners receive only delivery-related information (pickup address, drop address, and route). Their per-trip earnings are communicated within the partner app and are not shared with customers or restaurants.

EATRIX reserves the right to optimise pricing, delivery allocation, and operational costs through automated systems and internal algorithms. The outputs of these systems — such as the delivery fee or assigned delivery partner — may be shared with the relevant parties only to the extent necessary to fulfil the transaction. The underlying logic, model parameters, and cost structures remain proprietary and are not disclosed.

4.5 With Legal Authorities

We may disclose your information to government bodies, law enforcement agencies, or courts if:

  • Required by a valid legal order, warrant, or statutory obligation
  • Necessary to protect the safety of users, the public, or the integrity of our platform
  • Required under the Information Technology Act, 2000 or any other applicable Indian law

We will, to the extent legally permissible, notify affected users of such disclosures.

5. Data Protection and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented technical and organisational safeguards proportionate to the sensitivity of the data we hold.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS / TLS encryption.
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data stored in our databases is encrypted using industry-standard algorithms.
  • Access controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorised EATRIX personnel who need it to perform their job. All access is logged.
  • Payment security: Payment flows are handled end-to-end by PCI-DSS certified payment gateways. We do not store full payment credentials.
  • Regular audits: We conduct periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments of our infrastructure.
  • Incident response: In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the relevant regulatory authorities in accordance with applicable law.

While we implement strong protections, no system is 100% immune from security threats. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your EATRIX account and report any suspicious activity to support@eatrix.in immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have meaningful rights over your personal data under Indian data protection law. We have made it straightforward to exercise them.

7.1 Right to Access

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. We will provide this within 30 days of a verified request. You can begin this process from Settings > Privacy > Download My Data in the EATRIX app.

7.2 Right to Correction

If any of your personal information is inaccurate or incomplete, you may update it directly in the app (Settings > Profile) or contact us at support@eatrix.in. We will make corrections within 7 business days.

7.3 Right to Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data. To do so, go to Settings > Account > Delete My Account, or email support@eatrix.in. We will complete deletion within 30 days, subject to our legal retention obligations (e.g., financial records required by law).

7.4 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on your consent to process data (e.g., marketing communications or optional location sharing), you may withdraw consent at any time through the app's notification and privacy settings. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before consent was withdrawn.

7.5 Right to Grievance Redressal

If you believe your data rights have been violated, you may raise a formal grievance with our Data Protection Officer at support@eatrix.in. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to escalate to the Data Protection Board of India as established under the DPDP Act, 2023.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

EATRIX uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and mobile application to deliver and improve our services.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function (e.g., maintaining your login session, storing your cart). These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences such as language, saved addresses, and dietary filters.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users navigate the app and identify areas for improvement. This data is aggregated and anonymised.
  • Security cookies: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity during your session.

We do NOT use advertising or behavioural targeting cookies. We do not permit third-party advertisers to place cookies on our platform.

You can manage cookie preferences on our website via the Cookie Settings panel in the footer. On mobile, you can reset your advertising ID or limit ad tracking through your device settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will affect your ability to use the platform.

9. Third-Party Services

To operate efficiently, EATRIX integrates with carefully selected third-party services. We choose partners who meet high data security standards and share only the minimum data necessary.

Key third-party integrations include:

  • Maps and Navigation (e.g., Google Maps Platform): Used to display restaurant locations, provide delivery route guidance, and show live order tracking on the customer's screen. Google receives approximate location data for map rendering. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Payment Gateways (e.g., Razorpay / PayU): Process all financial transactions on the platform. These gateways are PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. EATRIX does not handle raw payment credentials. Governed by the gateway's own privacy and security terms.
  • SMS and Notification Services: Used to send OTPs, order confirmations, and delivery alerts. Only your phone number or device token is shared for this purpose.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted on reputable cloud providers with data centres in India (or compliant international locations with appropriate data transfer safeguards).
  • Analytics Tools: We use anonymised, aggregated analytics to measure app performance. No personally identifiable data is shared with analytics providers.

We conduct periodic due diligence on all third-party partners to ensure they maintain appropriate data protection standards. We do not permit third parties to use data shared with them for their own marketing purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

EATRIX is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18 years. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at support@eatrix.in. We will investigate and promptly delete any such data upon verification.

Restaurant partners and delivery partners must be adults (18 years or older) to register on our platform. Identity verification during onboarding helps enforce this requirement.
