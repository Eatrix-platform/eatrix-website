Welcome to EATRIX Technologies LLP ("EATRIX", "we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, with whom we share it, and the rights available to users of our platform.
1

Introduction

Welcome to EATRIX Technologies LLP ("EATRIX", "we", "our", or "us"). We operate a food delivery platform connecting customers, restaurants, and delivery partners across Hyderabad and other cities in India.

We understand that your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains in plain language:

  • What personal information we collect and why.
  • How we use and protect that information.
  • With whom we share it and the strict limits we place on that sharing.
  • Your rights as a user of our platform.

By using the EATRIX mobile application or website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our platform.

We are committed to handling your data responsibly, transparently, and in accordance with applicable Indian data protection principles, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

This policy applies to all users of our platform, including customers placing orders, restaurant partners fulfilling them, and delivery partners completing deliveries.

2

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver our services. Below is a clear breakdown of what we collect from each group of users.

2.1 Personal Data

We collect basic personal details that help us create and manage your account:

  • Full name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile photograph (optional)

For restaurant partners, we also collect business name, FSSAI licence number, and authorised contact person details. For delivery partners, we collect government-issued ID proof and vehicle information for onboarding and compliance purposes.

2.2 Location Data

Location data is central to how EATRIX works. Here is exactly how we use it:

  • Customers: We collect your delivery address and, if you permit, real-time device location to suggest nearby restaurants and provide accurate delivery tracking.
  • Delivery partners: We collect live GPS location while a delivery is active. This data is used solely to match assignments, update customers on delivery progress, and calculate route efficiency.
  • Restaurants: We use the registered address to show your listing to nearby customers.

You can disable location permissions on your device at any time, though some features may not function without it.

2.3 Transaction Data

To process and record orders, we collect:

  • Order items, quantities, and special instructions
  • Order timestamps and delivery address
  • Order status updates and delivery confirmation
  • Payment method type (such as UPI, card, wallet). We do not store full card numbers or CVV codes.
  • Partial transaction references, such as the last 4 digits of a card or UPI handle, for order history and refund purposes
2.4 Device and App Usage Data

To improve our platform and diagnose technical issues, we automatically collect:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and network type
  • App usage patterns such as screens visited, time spent, and features used
  • Crash reports and error logs

This technical data does not directly identify you and is used in aggregated, anonymised form for platform analytics.

3

How We Use Your Information

We use your data for specific, legitimate purposes. We never sell your data to third parties.

3.1 Order Processing
  • Accept, confirm, and communicate your orders
  • Transmit order details to the relevant restaurant
  • Assign a delivery partner to your order
  • Send real-time notifications about order status
  • Generate digital receipts and invoices
3.2 Delivery Tracking
  • Provide a live map showing your order's progress
  • Estimate accurate delivery times
  • Optimise delivery routes to reduce wait times
  • Confirm successful delivery at your address
3.3 Customer Support
  • Identify your account and the relevant order quickly
  • Investigate and resolve complaints, disputes, and refund requests
  • Maintain records of resolutions for quality improvement
3.4 Fraud Prevention and Safety
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent orders, fake accounts, or payment abuse
  • Protect delivery partners from unsafe delivery locations
  • Flag suspicious activity on restaurant partner accounts

Our fraud detection systems use automated logic with human review for enforcement actions.

3.5 Platform Improvement
  • Identify and fix bugs or crashes
  • Improve the speed and reliability of the app
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users
  • Plan new features and improvements

Where we conduct A/B testing or feature experiments, we do so with anonymised user cohorts and never use it to discriminate between users in pricing or service quality.

3.6 Pricing and Order Data

When you place an order, EATRIX uses your order details, delivery address, and location data to calculate the total amount payable. This total is shown before you confirm payment and includes the item subtotal, applicable taxes, and the delivery fee.

  • Total payable amount: The delivery fee shown to customers is a single bundled figure. Internal cost components are not disclosed separately.
  • Restaurant payouts: Restaurants receive only the information relevant to their payout and do not receive details of the delivery fee or internal platform cost components.
  • Delivery partner earnings: Delivery partners receive only the information needed to complete their assignment. Their earnings are communicated separately in the partner app.
  • Pricing logic: EATRIX does not share its internal pricing algorithms, cost models, or fee structures with third parties, except where strictly required for operations.
The total amount shown at checkout is the final amount you pay. No hidden charges are applied after confirmation.
4

Sharing of Information

We follow a strict need-to-know principle. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

4.1 With Restaurants

When you place an order, the restaurant receives only:

  • Your first name
  • Order items, quantities, and customisation notes
  • Estimated pickup time

Restaurants do not receive your phone number, full address, email, payment details, or order history from other restaurants.

4.2 With Delivery Partners

The assigned delivery partner receives only what is needed to complete the delivery:

  • Your first name
  • Delivery address or real-time drop-pin
  • Your phone number during the active delivery only
  • Restaurant pickup location

Once delivery is completed, the delivery partner's access to your personal information is revoked.

4.3 With Payment Gateways

Payments are processed by certified third-party payment service providers. We share only what is necessary to process the specific transaction:

  • Transaction amount and order reference ID
  • Payment method selected by the user

EATRIX does not receive or store your full card numbers, CVV, or net banking credentials.

4.4 Pricing and Internal Cost Data
  • Payment gateways receive only the total transaction amount and order reference.
  • Analytics providers receive only anonymised, aggregated data.
  • Restaurants receive only their payout amount.
  • Delivery partners receive only delivery-related information and their own payout data inside the partner app.
4.5 With Legal Authorities

We may disclose information to government bodies, law enforcement agencies, or courts if:

  • Required by a valid legal order, warrant, or statutory obligation
  • Necessary to protect the safety of users, the public, or the integrity of our platform
  • Required under applicable Indian law
5

Data Protection and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented technical and organisational safeguards proportionate to the sensitivity of the data we hold.

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS / TLS encryption.
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data stored in our databases is encrypted using industry-standard algorithms.
  • Access controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorised personnel and all access is logged.
  • Payment security: Payment flows are handled end-to-end by PCI-DSS certified payment gateways.
  • Regular audits: We conduct periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments.
  • Incident response: In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and the relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law.

While we implement strong protections, no system is completely immune from security threats. Please use a strong password and report suspicious activity to support@eatrix.in.

6

Data Retention Policy

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

Data Category Retention Period Reason
Account and profile data Duration of account + 2 years after deletion Support, dispute resolution
Order history 5 years Legal and tax compliance
Active delivery location Until delivery is complete Operational only
Payment transaction references 8 years Financial records requirement
Device and usage logs 12 months Platform analytics and debugging
Support communications 3 years Dispute resolution
Deleted account data 30 days (anonymised backup) Accidental deletion recovery

After the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised so that it can no longer be associated with any individual.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

EATRIX uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and mobile application to deliver and improve our services.

  • Essential cookies: Required for platform functionality.
  • Functional cookies: Remember preferences such as language and saved addresses.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users navigate the app.
  • Security cookies: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity during your session.

We do not use advertising or behavioural targeting cookies and do not permit third-party advertisers to place cookies on our platform.

Third-Party Services
