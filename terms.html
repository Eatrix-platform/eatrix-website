Pricing and Payments

EATRIX is committed to honest, straightforward pricing. What you see at checkout is exactly what you pay — no surprises, no hidden charges. This section explains how charges are presented to Customers, and how EATRIX's commercial arrangements with Restaurants and Delivery Partners work separately.

6.1 Food Item Price

The price of each food item is set solely by the Restaurant. EATRIX does not influence, mark up, or control menu pricing. The price displayed on the Platform for any item reflects the price set by the Restaurant, inclusive of any GST that the Restaurant is required to charge under applicable law.

6.2 Delivery Fee

Customers are charged a single, unified Delivery Fee for each order. This fee is a bundled amount that covers all costs associated with getting your order to you, including delivery distance, logistics operations, and platform infrastructure required to coordinate and complete the delivery. The Delivery Fee is not itemised into its component parts on the customer-facing checkout screen; it is presented as a single, consolidated charge.

The Delivery Fee varies by order and is calculated based on the distance between the Restaurant and the delivery location. As a general guide:

  • For deliveries up to 3 km, a standard base Delivery Fee applies.
  • For deliveries beyond 3 km, an incremental per-km charge is added to the base fee.
  • The exact Delivery Fee applicable to your order will always be displayed at checkout before you confirm and pay.
Note: The Delivery Fee is not a commission charged to the Restaurant. It is a customer-facing charge for the delivery service. The Delivery Fee is a bundled charge and its internal components are not itemised separately to Customers. EATRIX reserves the right to optimise pricing, delivery allocation, and operational costs through automated systems and internal algorithms.
6.3 What You See is What You Pay

Before confirming any order, Customers will see a clear order summary that includes:

  • The subtotal of food items selected;
  • The applicable Delivery Fee for the order;
  • GST and other taxes, where separately applicable and required to be shown under law; and
  • The total payable amount before order confirmation.

There are no charges added after checkout. No surcharges, service fees, or additional levies will be applied to a Customer's order beyond what is displayed at the time of confirmation.

6.4 Taxes Displayed to Customers

Where required by the Goods and Services Tax Act, 2017, or any other applicable legislation, GST and other statutory taxes will be displayed as separate line items in the Customer's order summary. The food item prices shown by Restaurants may be inclusive of GST depending on how the Restaurant has configured their listings. In all cases, the total payable amount shown before confirmation includes all applicable taxes.

6.5 Restaurant Pricing Arrangements (Not Visible to Customers)

EATRIX operates on a zero commission on food order value model for Restaurants. No percentage-based commission is deducted from the value of any Customer's order. Instead, the Platform operates on a subscription and fixed service fee model — Restaurants pay EATRIX through a monthly subscription fee and a fixed per-order service fee. These are business-to-business charges agreed upon between EATRIX and the Restaurant, and they do not appear on the Customer's order summary or receipt in any form.

For details of EATRIX's fee structure applicable to Restaurants and Delivery Partners, please refer to Sections 7.2 and 7.4 respectively.
